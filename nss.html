NATIONAL SERVICE SCHEME UNIT



The National Service Scheme was started by Government of India, Ministry of Education & Culture, Government of India, in collaboration with the state government in the year1969, in 37 selected Universities. Right from its beginning, Mumbai University has been participating in this scheme. At present, the scheme is run by the Ministry of Youth affairs & sports in Central government & department of Higher & Technical education in state government. N.S.S. Unit of our college is one of the largest forums.
N.S.S. unit has conducts following activities at local, regional, state & national level: cleanliness drive, Beach cleaning activity, Blood Donation, Disaster Management Workshop, Cloth Donation Drive, Road Safety Activity, Plastic Mukt Abhiyan.
